About Hakimerad

A new vision for radiology in Ethiopia.

Our Story

Hakimerad is a professional venture startup company founded in 2025 by Dr. Dawit Muluneh, a practicing certified radiologist, to address the growing radiologist shortage impacting healthcare facilities nationwide. As a teleradiology service provider, we offer flexible outsourcing solutions for healthcare providers of all sizes.

We specialize in interpreting and diagnosing medical images submitted to us through our secure, state-of-the-art online platform, ensuring that every clinic and hospital can have access to expert radiological services, regardless of their location.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We are committed to revolutionizing radiology in Ethiopia by leveraging AI and expert radiologists to improve patient outcomes.

Our Vision

To become the trusted teleradiology partner for every clinic and hospital in Ethiopia.
